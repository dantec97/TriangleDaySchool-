Meet Tobey

Grade 4  ·  Triangle Day School

What makes Triangle Day School feel like home?

All the teachers know my name. Even the older kids say hi to me in the hallway.
scroll

When Tobey first came to TDS, he was nervous about being the new kid. It didn't take long before classmates invited him into games at recess, teachers remembered the little things that made him unique, and the school started to feel like a second home.
